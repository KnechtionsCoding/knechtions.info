About Hans Knecht

Hans Knecht has been working in the IT world for five years, spending time doing everything from front-line tech support to administering and rebuilding company IT infrastructure. He hs also worked in the business world doing evyerthing frombeing a receptionist to starting and running a small business. This has lead to an ability to speak not only the language of technology but also the language of business.

Hans Knecht is combining real world experience and academic studies by going to BYU to get a Masters of Science in Information Systems and a Bachelors of Science in Information Systems and Information Technology.

Hans Knecht speaks English and Swedish fluently and has spent time living on three different continents. Having traveled all over the world for business and pleasure he brings a very special perspective to business and brings with him many contacts around the world.
